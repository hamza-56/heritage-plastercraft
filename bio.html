About Us

Who we are

Heritage Plastercraft is built on generations of traditional plasterwork knowledge, passed down through skilled craftsmen dedicated to preserving the beauty and integrity of historical interiors.

From heritage buildings and period homes to bespoke contemporary projects, our work reflects a deep respect for classical methods combined with a commitment to precision and quality.

Every piece we create — whether a restored cornice, a hand‑cast ceiling rose, or a fully reconstructed interior — is crafted with care, authenticity, and an unwavering attention to detail.
