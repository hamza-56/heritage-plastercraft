Our Services

What We Do

From meticulous restoration to bespoke commissions, we offer a comprehensive range of traditional plasterwork services for heritage properties, period homes, and contemporary interiors.

Restoration & Conservation

Careful repair and reinstatement of damaged or deteriorating plasterwork in listed buildings and heritage properties. We work sensitively to preserve original character while ensuring structural integrity.

Cornices & Coving

Period-correct cornices and coving profiles, whether matching existing designs for reinstatement or creating new runs from traditional moulds. Every detail is true to the original craftsmanship.

Ceiling Roses

Hand-cast ceiling roses in a variety of period styles — from delicate Georgian designs to ornate Victorian centrepieces. Each piece is individually crafted and finished to the highest standard.

Mouldings & Architraves

Decorative plaster mouldings, panel mouldings, dado rails, and architraves made using traditional methods. Available in standard period profiles or custom designs to your specification.

Fibrous Plasterwork

Lightweight fibrous plaster elements pre-cast in the workshop and fitted on site. Ideal for complex decorative schemes, columns, arches, and large-scale ornamental features.

Bespoke Commissions

Custom plasterwork designed and created to your vision. From contemporary interpretations of classical motifs to entirely original designs — we bring your ideas to life in plaster.
