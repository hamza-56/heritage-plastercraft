Welcome

At Heritage Plastercraft, we specialise in the restoration, conservation, and creation of fine plasterwork. With decades of experience, we bring traditional craftsmanship together with modern precision to deliver exceptional results for heritage properties, period homes, and bespoke interior projects.

Whether you require ornate ceiling roses, cornices, mouldings, or complete restoration of historical interiors, our work reflects a commitment to authenticity, detail, and timeless elegance.
